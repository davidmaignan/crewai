FinTech Plus announced stellar Q2 2023 earnings with $125 million in revenue, a 25% year-over-year increase. The gross profit margin was 58% and EBITDA reached $37.5 million. Net income for the quarter was $16 million, up from $10 million in Q2 2022. Expansion of high-yield savings and a new RoboAdvisor platform increased the total addressable market. Investment in asset-backed securities and $25 million in AAA-rated corporate bonds aimed to enhance risk-adjusted returns. Total assets were $1.5 billion, with liabilities at $900 million and equity at $600 million. Debt to equity ratio is 1.5 and LTVCAC ratio is 3.5x. Organic user growth continues with lowered acquisition costs and increased lifetime value. Risk management is strong with a 99% confidence level on the value-at-risk model. A positive forecast for Q3 includes revenue expectations of $135 million and the IPO of subsidiary Pay Plus to raise $200 million.
